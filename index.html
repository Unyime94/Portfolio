01.About Me

Hello! My name is Unyime and I enjoy creating things that live on the web. My interest in web development started out as a curiosity to understand how the web works. I soon started experimenting with building user-interfaces with just HTML and CSS. As my interest deepened, I explored adding functionalities with Javascript, improving performance with React, including other frameworks and technologies. I create digital art too (scroll to see). Frontend Development has my heart because it intersects my love for creativity and problem-solving. I get excited when my work comes to life!
